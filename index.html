ABOUT

I'm a student at Kenzie Academy, enrolled in the UIE-4 Cohort. I'm a visual artist, mom and UX Engineer. My goal is to create meaningful and inspired designs, to share my abilities with a world of people who need great design that speaks to their audience. My hope is to show my daughters that they can set out to achieve anything they are willing to work hard for.

  • StyleList

    View

    StyleList is a foundation project that encompasses a feature-rich CRM for beauty professionals and scales to fit the user's needs. Appointment booking and inventory management as well as tracking client preferences are all part of one simple, intuitive process designed with beauty professionals in mind.

  • EventFull

    View

    EventFull is a capstone project designed to compete with Eventbrite and other event management apps. This project was developed with a team of four designers and features a range of tools for users who wish to plan or attend events and find others who are interested in the same types of activities. Allowing users to communicate with event planning teams and atendees sets EventFull apart from the competition.

  • Red Tide

    View

    A presentation requested by Sanibel Island to answer their request for research and potential solutions to the red tide crisis that was destroying thier local ecosystem. This presentation explains the causes of the irregular algea blooms and a few ways to prevent further damages.
